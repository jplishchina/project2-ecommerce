Shipping

Currently, we only ship within the continental United States, and hope to soon expand our services internationally. Typical delivery speed is 3-7 days from our Staten Island warehouse to your doorstep. Our standard shipping uses USPS Priority Shipping, which can be upgraded at checkout. Once we recieve an order, it is shipped from our warehouse within 48 hours, and you recieve a shipping confirmation email fit with tracking information. If you have any questions about Shipping, please contact us using the form below!

Returns/Exchanges

We maintain a 30-day return policy from the date of recieving your order. If you have any concerns or issues with the product recieved, please reach out to us using the form below or call us at our dedicated phone number (012) 345-6789. We always strive to keep our customers satisfied. You can return your item for a refund to your original payment method, or for store credit of equal value.

Some exceptions to our return policy:

  • Products must be returned in original product packaging, with all tags still attached.
  • Returned items must not have any signs of use or wear.
  • Clearanced items may not be returned.
  • If a discounted item is returned after the discount on the product has ended, the payment returned will reflect the price paid.

Initiating a Return:

To start a return, fill out the form below selecting "Return" as your reason for filing a ticket. In the ticket, please include your order number, the item being returned, the reason for return as well as preferred refund method. We will review the return within 24 hours, and email you a return shipping label. The refund will be processed when we recieve confirmation that the product has been shipped. We do not charge for return shipping, but please note that we charge a $10.00 restocking fee.

Return FAQs
  • When will I recieve my refund?
    Once the refund is processed (after the order is shipped back) the refund should return to your original payment method within 3-5 days. If your chosen refund is to store credit, the refund amount is credited to your account immediately after processing.
  • What if I never recieve my package?
    If your package has not arrived by the expected delivery date, please give the USPS a few extra days in case there were any delays. File a help ticket with us, and we will contact USPS and file a claim to find the product. If we are still unable to get the package delivered, we will refund you the full cost of the order, or if preferred, send you a replacement. We maintain responsibility for your package until it is marked as "delivered", or if you purchased Certified Mail, the package is signed for.
  • If you have any specific questions or concerns, please contact us.
